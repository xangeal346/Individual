Who Do We Serve?

Our non-profit organization has devoted its resources, efficiency and cooperation to undocumented students with a Hispanic/Latino background that come from low income families. We aim to ensure that these less fortunate students enroll in a high quality, low-cost four year higher institution, where they will be able obtain a college education, apply their skills in the American workforce and give back to their communities. We serve Hispanics/Latinos because they are attacked by nativists and have the lowest percentages of acquired occupations in high demanding jobs. Those who are hindered from continuing their education often turn to vices or instead low paying jobs that continue the cycle of being part of a low-income family. We want to break that cycle. We want to give those are willing to invest their time, dreams, and hope to contribute to society the same opportunity as everyone else. People migrate in search of a better life, not to remain in the same situation.
