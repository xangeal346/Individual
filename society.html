Legal Services for Immigrants

Empowering the Voices of the Future

We are a small creative non-profit organization in New York, founded in 2015. We are committed to empowering the underrepresented, urban Latino/Hispanic youth in the Bronx by providing them with the essential knowledge. We strive to highly motivate immigrants who, without financial aid, cannot afford to enroll in college. We want to lift some of their burdens by providing guidance all the step of the way, money and additional materials. As a result, the youth will be prepared for the oncoming life challenging financial decisions that will have to be dealt with because they have obtained a good paying job.
